# Factions

Factions let players group together, protect their ships and stations, declare wars, and organise with ranks and permissions.

## Creating or Joining a Faction

Press `H` to open the Faction Panel.

| Tab | Purpose |
|-----|---------|
| **Personal** | Create a new faction or manage your current membership |
| **Faction** | Edit your faction's description, news, and diplomacy |
| **Faction Hub** | Browse all factions on the server; join public ones |
| **Invites** | Accept pending invitations |

To create a faction: go to the **Personal** tab → **Create New Faction** → enter a name → OK.

To join: find a public faction in the **Faction Hub** and click join, or accept an invitation in the **Invites** tab.

## Faction Options

In the **Faction** tab → options:

| Option | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Public Faction | Anyone can join without an invitation |
| Declare War on Hostile Action | Auto-declares war if your faction is attacked |
| Consider Neutral Enemy | Treats all factionless entities as enemies |

## Ranks and Permissions

Every member has a **rank**. Ranks are configurable with custom names and permission sets. Click **Roles** in the Faction tab to manage them.

### Permission Types

| Permission | Grants |
|-----------|--------|
| **Edit** | Can edit faction description and post news |
| **Kick** | Can kick members of lower rank |
| **Invite** | Can invite new players |
| **Permission-Edit** | Can modify rank permissions — give only to trusted leaders |

To change a member's rank: click **Options** next to their name → select a rank.

## Diplomacy

From the **Faction Hub** tab, click the **Relation** button next to any other faction to:
- Declare war
- Send a peace offer
- Send an alliance offer

Peace and alliance offers must be accepted by the other faction's leaders.

### Personal Enemies

You can declare war against a **single player** rather than their whole faction. In the **Faction** tab → **Personal Enemies**.

## Faction Modules: Claiming Ships and Stations

To assign a ship or station to your faction:

1. Place a **Faction Module** on the structure.
2. Press `R` on it.
3. Enter your **faction signature** (visible in the faction panel).

While the faction module exists and is not destroyed, only you and your faction members can use that structure.

## Homebase

A station claimed by a faction can be designated as the faction's **Homebase** (only one per faction). The homebase has a major advantage:

> **The homebase and everything docked to it are invulnerable to damage.**

This makes the homebase the safest location for your most valuable assets. Choose its location and defences carefully — it is the heart of your faction.

## Faction Chat

Send messages only to faction members by typing `/f ` before your message in chat.
